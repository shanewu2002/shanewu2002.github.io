Shane Wu's Biography

About Me

My name is Shih-Hsuan Wu, you can also call me Shane. I am studying at the department of electrical engineering in National Tsing Hua University. Born on January 21, 2002 as an Aquarius, I am a person with diversity who usually think like an alien and have lots of hobbies.

Working Out

Since I am 20 years old now, I care about my appearance, which means I like to spend time working out in the gym. As I am not satisfied with my shape yet, I make every endeavor to go to gym every day.

Korean Dramas

After a heartbreaking breakup with my ex-girlfriend two years ago, I tried to ignore the pain by watching Korean dramas. I became a huge fan of Korean dramas. Although I enjoy romantic dramas, my favorite genre is always detective dramas. During the summer and winter vacations, the happiest thing for me is to watch dramas all day in bed.

Music

As a music lover, I enjoy a lot of type of music, such as Chinese pop songs, Pop, Kpop, and even songs composed by aboriginal singers. I also enjoy watching music producers’ reaction video to some popular compositions to get to know more about the details concerning the process of those incredible songs.

Anticipations

Studying electrical engineering is an exhausting and time-consuming journey. Though I have little interest in electrical engineering, I will thrive to retain perseverance until I graduate from NTHU. I believe that hard work will betray no one. As a result, I would like to take certain things seriously, looking forward to becoming a better person.

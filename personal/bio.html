Shane Wu's Biography

About Me

My name is Shih-Hsuan Wu. You can also call me Shane. I am studying in the Department of Electrical Engineering at National Tsing Hua University. As an Aquarius, I am a person with diversity and consider myself an alien who has lots of hobbies.

Working Out

Since I am a grown-up, I care more about my appearance, which means I like to spend time working out in the gym. As I am not satisfied with my shape yet, I make every endeavor to go to the gym every day.

Korean Dramas

After a heartbreaking breakup with my ex-girlfriend two years ago, I tried to ignore the pain by watching Korean dramas. Therefore, I became a huge fan of Korean dramas. Although I enjoy romantic dramas, my favorite genre is always detective dramas. During the summer and winter vacations, the happiest thing for me is watching dramas all day in bed.

Music

As a music lover, I enjoy a lot of types of music, such as Chinese pop songs, Pop, K-pop, and even songs composed by aboriginal singers. I also enjoy watching music producers’ reaction videos to some popular compositions to get to know more about the details concerning the process of those incredible songs.

Anticipations

Studying electrical engineering is an exhausting and time-consuming journey. Though I have little interest in electrical engineering, I will thrive to retain perseverance in order to graduate from NTHU since I believe that perseraerance betrays nobody. As for the future, I hope that I can keep maintaining the good personalities and I look forward to becoming a better person.
